Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 30, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our application ("n8n Social Automation") handles information when you use our service to post content to Facebook and Instagram.

2. Information We Collect

Our application:

  • Does NOT collect or store your personal information
  • Does NOT track your browsing activity
  • Does NOT sell or share your data with third parties

3. How We Use Facebook/Instagram API

Our application uses Facebook's Graph API to:

  • Authenticate your Facebook/Instagram account
  • Post content to your Facebook Pages and Instagram accounts
  • Access basic page information (Page ID, name)

All authentication is handled directly by Facebook. We do not store your login credentials.

4. Data Storage

We do not store any personal data on our servers. All data processing happens locally on your device through the n8n automation platform.

5. Third-Party Services

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Revoke access to your Facebook/Instagram account at any time through your Facebook settings
  • Delete any content posted through our application
  • Request information about how your data is processed

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information. All API communications use HTTPS encryption.
